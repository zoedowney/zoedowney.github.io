hello and welcome to my portfolio—

I'm zoë, a photographer based near london, uk. I document the mundane and everyday ephemera that seek digital preservation. I also write for my blog on cinema & contemporary media.

imagery is a concern across my work, augmented by the critical discourse that orbits it.

collected here are:

1) photo projects & series I'm assembling
2) film reviews & extended thought piecess

I graduated from goldsmiths college, london in 2020 with my masters in american literature & culture—with a specialism in american and japanese animation. I hold a first-class bachelor's in english (goldsmiths, 2017).
