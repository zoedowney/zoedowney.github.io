hello and welcome to my portfolio—

I'm zoë, a photographer based near london, uk. I document the mundane and everyday ephemera that seek digital preservation. I also write for my blog on cinema & contemporary media.

imagery is the concern across my work, augmented by the critical discourse that orbits it.

collected here are:

1) photo projects & series I'm assembling
2) film reviews & extended thought pieces

view my side project 'shoe sighting'/ @shoesighting whereby I caputure and curate footwear street photography. details interest me; micro features over macro or large-scale subjects.

read my contribution to pop sonality blog, where I shared my 10 tips for aspiring photographers.
