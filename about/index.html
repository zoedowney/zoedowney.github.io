my process, ongoing projects & features:

imagery is the concern across my work, paired with the critical discourse that orbits it. in taking photos, my present focus is on abstract details of still-life and subtler movements of street photography; scaling-down my approach and documenting the smaller—but significant—ordinary.

view my project 'shoe sighting' (@shoesighting), whereby I capture and curate photography that features footwear in beautiful or unusual ways. this work is inspired by my time at ual: london college of fashion where—in 2013—I spent a brief but experimental year studying to become a cordwainer.

read my post on pop sonality blog where I share my 10 tips for aspiring photographers.

a short bio:

my love for photography was sparked by my dad's interest in lomography and film, and later rekindled during a trip to japan in 2019.

I earned my first-class bachelor's in english from goldsmiths, university in 2017. in 2020, I graduated with a master's in american literature & culture. I specialised in ecocriticism surrounding american and japanese animation.
