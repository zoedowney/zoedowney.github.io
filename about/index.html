I'm zoë, a photographer based on the fringes of london, uk. I document the mundane and everyday ephemera that seek digital preservation. I also write on cinema & contemporary media.

imagery is the concern across my work, paired with the critical discourse that orbits it. presently my focus is on the details of street photography and still-life; micro features in the ordinary over macro, grand or large-scale subjects.

presented here are:

1) assembled photo projects & series
2) film reviews & extended thought pieces.

view my featured project 'shoe sighting' (@shoesighting), whereby I caputure and curate footwear street photography.
