hello and welcome to my portfolio—

I'm zoë, a photographer based near london, uk. I document the mundane and everyday ephemera that seek digital preservation. I also write for my blog on cinema & contemporary media.

collected here are photo series I'm presently working on, along with film reviews & thought pieces.

I graduated from goldsmiths college, london in 2022 with my masters in american literature & culture—with a specialism in american and japanese animation. I hold a first-class bachelor's in english (goldsmiths, 2017).
